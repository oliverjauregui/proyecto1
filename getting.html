Getting Started

  • Complete your registration form and send it to us.
  • After we recieve your information, you will be contacted via
    e-mail to confirm the time of your first class(free of cost) and
    recieve our Skype Number.
  • In your first class we will determine your Spanish level.
  • We will also ensure that Skype and Yuuguu are working well on
    your computer.
  • Please read the Terms and Conditions before your begin the classes.

Setting up

  • After you have signed up and paid for the course level of your
    choice, we will schedule you for your classes according to your
    convenience.
  • You will receive this information via email.
  • We will provide you with all the material to be used in classes.
  • The duration of each class will be an hour(60 minutes)
  • For your first scheduled class, your teacher will contact
    your by Skype.

What do you need?

    Basic Computer Requirements:

  • Broadband internet connection(ADSL, xDSL, cable)
  • Operating systems: Windows XP or Windows 7/Mac-OS or Linux
  • Operational sound system including a microphone, speakers
    or headset.
  • Skype and Yuuguu Free Programs (download by clicking
    on the logos)
